Who We Are

In today's world, almost every decision we make has an impact on the environment.
But understanding which choices are most important for protecting the planet can be overwhelming.
That's why we, the students studying digital design in MYP 4, have come together with a mission.
We want to create a website that shines a light on the environmental issues we face and offers practical solutions to address them.

People in the photo: Mannat Alwa, Rithanyaa TK, Rishit Nagpal, Sonakshi Chavan, Tanishka Sathe, Tushant Avudurthi
Our facilitator: Ms. Mridula

Purpose


We believe that everyone should have access to information about how to make a positive impact on the environment.
By sharing our knowledge and passion for sustainability through this website, we hope to empower others to take action in their own lives.
Whether it's reducing waste, conserving energy, or supporting eco-friendly practices, we want to inspire change and make a difference for our planet's future.

Goals

Our goal is to make environmental awareness and action accessible to everyone, regardless of their background
or expertise. Through our website, we will provide clear and simple explanations of environmental issues, along with practical tips and
resources for making sustainable choices. By breaking down complex topics into easy-to-understand information, we hope to inspire and empower
individuals to join us in protecting the planet for future generations.

Contributions

We believe that collective action is key to addressing environmental challenges. That's why we invite you
to explore our website, learn more about the issues, and discover ways to make a positive impact in your daily life. Whether
it's through small changes in your habits or advocating for broader systemic change, every
action counts. Together, we can create a more sustainable and resilient world for ourselves and for future generations to come.
