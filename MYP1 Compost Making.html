MYP 1 Compost Making

Compost is a nutrient-rich soil conditioner created through the natural decomposition of organic materials like kitchen scraps and yard waste.Composting is a natural process turning kitchen and garden waste into nutrient-rich soil. Use a mix of browns (carbon-rich, e.g., leaves) and greens (nitrogen-rich, e.g., kitchen scraps), maintain moisture, turn the pile regularly, and harvest when it's dark and crumbly. Avoid meat, dairy, and diseased plants. Composting reduces landfill waste and creates valuable fertilizer.
